Part II: The Transformation

The Alchemy
of Aging

owl
Oak Interaction

We utilize 500L French oak barrels with "blonde" toasting. This prevents the wood from masking the mineral precision of the schist, adding only a whisper of spice and structural breath.

thermostat
Static Gravity

Temperature is maintained at a constant 14°C within the natural granite walls of the cellar. The lack of vibration allows the phenolic compounds to weave together in absolute stillness.

hourglass_empty
The Tertiary Bloom

After 24 months in wood, the wine rests for another year in the bottle. Here, the primary fruit undergoes molecular alchemy, emerging as notes of tobacco, graphite, and dried violets.
